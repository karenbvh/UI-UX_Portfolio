Objective

The goal is to create an experience that gives the user the feeling of "This looks fun and I see myself going." In this case study I am going to use an independent perfomance company "N.A.P. Films" first movie feature "Welcome to Midori's House." The company needs a quick and effective booking syte that engages with their customers.


I realize that inviting customers' to go out and watch independent movies is a difficult task because they live in a world of streaming popular movies at home.

Research

I started off with a little marketing research, I read an academic article reviewing the online movie ticket company Fandago, "Online and on the Go" while at the same time exploring the app itself. Here are the things I learned:

Research

  • The box office reported sales of 10% every year, while Fandago has seen 57% year over year growth.
  • More users are ordering movie tickets using voice commands via Siri and Google now.
  • "Scanerless Ticketing" mobile feature allows to speed up the process of entering the movie theater by swiping "tear your ticket" at the entrance.

Fandango

  • It is hard to update or fix information as you go through the check out process.
  • Exposing organized content.
  • Events market

It is important to look at consumer's online shopping behavior. For that I kept in mind that many companies provide trial samples to potential customers to stimulate intention, purchasing, and repurchasing behaviors "freemium" a strategy has been recognized as a sucessful business model for various online companies.

Conclusion

I learned that for independent movies it is best to rely on costumer royalty and to give them an easy tool to plan their tickets and experience. However, users wants to receive any sort of reward or discount for making a purchase.

Also, the branding was also enhanced in the design by using the companies colours and its upcoming product.

A market strategy it is important to remind users that they are the most importat aspect of the business and should be rewarded with quick tools and a reward system.
